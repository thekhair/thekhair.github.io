Vision & Mission

The Kashmir Hub for Artificial Intelligence Research (KHAIR) will be established as a premier research institution committed to exploring the full spectrum of possibilities in ethical and cutting-edge artificial intelligence research. The KHAIR is dedicated to achieving technological self-reliance and inclusive growth for the region. Its mission is built on two core pillars: first, AI for Kashmiri Language Preservation, which focuses on building the world's most comprehensive Kashmiri Language Corpus and developing essential tools like ASR, TTS, and Machine Translation to digitize the local language; and second, AI for a Healthier Kashmir, which creates local solutions by establishing the J&K Health Data Atlas for data-driven insights and developing AI-Augmented Diagnostics to extend specialist care, particularly to remote health centers. The KHAIR ultimately seeks to build human capital by fostering academic integration and entrepreneurship by driving innovation through purpose-driven AI research and development

The Vision

To create a world where artificial intelligence acts as an augmentation tool for human creativity and problem-solving, driving global well-being and technological equity.

Core Mission

To conduct foundational and applied research in AI, focusing on transparency, ethics, and long-term societal impact, while fostering a diverse, collaborative research ecosystem.

AI for Kashmiri Language

To preserve and empower the rich linguistic heritage of Kashmir, KHAIR is committed to developing advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) and speech-based technologies for Kashmiri and other local languages. By building comprehensive datasets of text and spoken language, we aim to create tools such as automatic speech recognition (ASR), text-to-speech (TTS), and machine translation (MT) systems tailored to the unique phonetics, grammar, and dialects of the region. These technologies will not only make digital content accessible to all, including those with limited literacy or visual impairments, but also foster cultural preservation, enable voice-enabled interfaces for public services, education, and healthcare, and bridge communication gaps between local languages and global digital ecosystems.

AI for Healthcare

KHAIR will leverage the power of artificial intelligence to transform healthcare in Jammu & Kashmir by building AI models trained on local, region-specific data. By analyzing electronic health records, diagnostic reports, and medical imaging from the region, these models will be designed to identify unique patterns and risk factors in diseases such as cardiovascular conditions, chronic kidney disease, diabetes, and infectious illnesses. This localized approach will enable predictive analytics, early detection, and AI-augmented diagnostic tools that are tailored to the population‚Äôs specific genetics, environment, and lifestyle, ultimately improving public health outcomes and empowering healthcare providers in remote and underserved areas..

AI for Digital Safety

KHAIR will harness the power of artificial intelligence to enhance digital privacy and combat cyberbullying in Jammu & Kashmir. By developing AI-driven tools that monitor, detect, and flag harmful online behavior, we will create safer digital spaces for both men and women. These systems will leverage natural language processing and pattern recognition to identify harassment, offensive content, and privacy breaches in real time, while respecting individual data rights. Additionally, AI will support educational interventions and personalized alerts, empowering users to protect their personal information, report abuse, and engage online responsibly, thereby fostering a more secure and inclusive digital environment.

Robotics & Embodied AI

KHAIR will explore Robotics and Embodied AI at a small scale by developing compact, low-cost robotic platforms that can learn and interact with their environment. Using modular hardware, open-source software, and simulation tools, we will experiment with perception, navigation, and task execution in controlled settings. These small-scale robots will serve as testbeds for AI algorithms, allowing researchers and students to prototype, iterate, and refine intelligent behaviors before scaling up to more complex systems. This approach will make advanced robotics research accessible, practical, and cost-effective for local innovation.

Virtual Speech Therapist (AI)

This project aims to develop the Virtual Speech Therapist (VST), an agentic, AI-powered workflow designed for automated stuttering assessment and personalized therapy planning. VST integrates deep learning-based stuttering classification and multi-agent large language model (LLM) reasoning to support evidence-based clinical decision-making. The workflow begins with the acquisition and feature extraction of patient speech samples, followed by robust classification of stuttering types. Building on these outputs, VST initiates an agentic reasoning process in which specialized LLM agents autonomously generate, critique, and iteratively refine individualized therapy plans. A dedicated critic agent evaluates all generated therapy plans to ensure clinical safety, methodological soundness, and alignment with peer-reviewed evidence and established professional guidelines. The resulting output is a comprehensive, patient-specific therapy draft intended for clinician review. Incorporating clinician feedback, the system then produces a finalized therapy plan suitable for patient delivery, thereby maintaining a clinician-in-the-loop paradigm. A functional prototype has already been developed and evaluated by international clinicians. Their expert assessment confirms that VST consistently generates high-quality, evidence-based therapy recommendations. These findings demonstrate the system‚Äôs potential to augment clinical workflows, reduce clinician burden, and improve therapeutic outcomes for individuals with speech impairments.

AI for Agriculture and Plant Science

KHAIR will harness AI for Agriculture and Plant Omics, leveraging machine learning to analyze genomic, transcriptomic, and metabolomic data from local crops. By integrating these multi-layered biological datasets, AI will help identify traits for higher yield, disease resistance, and climate adaptability, tailored to the unique agro-climatic conditions of Jammu & Kashmir. Small-scale sensor networks and imaging systems will monitor plant health and growth in real time, enabling data-driven decision making for farmers and researchers, while fostering sustainable and precision agriculture practices in the region.

Abstract: Virtual Speech Therapist Agent

We present SpeechTherapyAgent, an AI-powered virtual speech therapist designed to provide personalized and supervised therapy for individuals with speech disorders. The system operates within a clinician-in-the-loop framework, ensuring that therapy sessions are both adaptive to patient needs and continuously monitored by healthcare professionals.

Our approach combines real-time speech analysis with machine learning algorithms to detect articulation errors, fluency issues, and prosodic abnormalities. The system generates personalized exercises that adapt based on patient progress, while providing clinicians with detailed analytics and intervention capabilities.

Experimental results demonstrate significant improvements in treatment adherence and speech accuracy compared to traditional therapy methods, while maintaining high safety standards through continuous clinician oversight.
